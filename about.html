Our Story

Joseton Construction was founded with a simple mission: to bring quality, reliability, and trust back into the world of construction.

What started as a small local crew has now grown into a full-scale operation, proudly serving homes and businesses across Botswana. Over the years, we've built not just structures, but lasting relationships with our clients.

Our journey began with a commitment to craftsmanship and integrity. Today, we're proud to be one of Botswana's most trusted construction firms, known for delivering exceptional results on every project we undertake.

Our Mission

We believe that a great building isn't just made of bricks and cement — it's built with care, planning, and passion.

Our mission is to deliver excellent work that lasts, while making our clients feel informed, respected, and confident every step of the way.

Our Journey

2010 - Humble Beginnings

Founded with just a team of 5 passionate builders and one small truck. Our first project was a residential extension in Gaborone.

2014 - First Major Project

Awarded our first commercial project – a shopping complex in Francistown. Expanded our team to 25 skilled professionals.

2018 - National Recognition

Received Botswana Construction Excellence Award for our work on the modern Maun Community Center.

2023 - Expanding Horizons

Currently employing over 120 construction professionals across 4 regional offices, working on projects nationwide.
